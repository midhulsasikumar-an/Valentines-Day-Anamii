🧸 Teddy Day

Because love should feel safe.

Whenever the world feels too loud… Whenever your brain won’t stop overthinking… Whenever you pretend you’re “fine” but I know you’re not… I wish I could just teleport there. Not in a superhero way. Just in a “sit next to you and annoy you until you smile” way. If you’re holding that teddy… Just imagine it’s me. Yes.Me. Except less fluffy.And slightly more handsome. And definitely more obsessed with you. You don’t always have to be strong.You don’t always have to handle everything alone. If I could,I’d sit next to you on your worst days. Not to fix everything. Because honestly,I probably can’t. But I can stay. I can listen.I can hold your hand. And if life ever feels cold… I hope I’m the idiot you come to for warmth.

Day 4 of Valentine Week 🖤
